obsession

all day it runs through my mind my latest obsession something that i want something that i need and it's all that i can think about it works its way into nearly every conversation into every aspect of life it becomes my reason for existing most don't get it many will never understand they view it from a miopic lens of "oh wow, that's great" or they take my words at face value and that's dangerous for me it embellishes my obsessive thinking making it more real more attainable i get tunnel vision and ignore the flaws begging for it to destroy me. whether or not it's healthy for me; the red flags blend into my crazed mind all day i dream about it every night i'm delusional i don't deserve this i'll never get it i drink i get even more crazed but i still drink and jesus fucking christ it gets worse everyone knows it's my identity because it's all i want it's all i need i'm laying in bed all day holy shit it's all i need begging for it to kill me it's all i fucking need maybe if i just drink enough i'll be lucky enough to make a mistake and ruin it all because then instead of obsessing over hypotheticals i'd have a real answer something i need so badly but i'm not good enough why would anyone want to deal with this why would anyone love me it's all i need the beauty in all of this is that my obsession makes me fiercely loyal to the point where it's all i need i take my pill i take a drink and the intrusive thoughts dissipate until the solace leaves me then immediately i remember every thing i said why did i say that why did i say that then i have to explain to my friends that evan hates me because of something riley said to grace but evan doesn't even fucking know riley or grace in fact, i don't even think they live in the same state and alex wore green on thursday but it was the 19th, which was a bad day in june and that's why she'll never love me the difference between this and pure social psychosis is that i know it's irrational but i still compulsively beg for reassurance "am i dying?" "do you hate me?" the answer is always yes until they say no and to those without a grasp on it compulsions never make sense but these questions don't leave my head if they go unanswered because it's all i need it's all i fucking need.
