my greatest fear

alphabetize your memoirs the thousands of letters you've written to people who don't care anymore life used to seem so simple too simple in fact so simple you pushed them out of it now life is EXCITING so exciting that everyone you meet is more interesting than the last you don't even remember their names anymore and you're self-destructive, burning yourself with every cigarette pledging to quit when the right person comes around but, hey, maybe they smoke too maybe they don't maybe their vices will be worse than the bottle and pack at the same time, maybe they'll be an avid church-goer not sure how you'll manage that, alcoholic, nicotine-addicted, bipolar you but someone out there is sure to love you the way you are now you swear up and down "i'm better! i promise!" but to those who never saw you suffer well, at least the same suffering as back then, you're just you that's alright i'm alright sometimes i wonder if i wear my heart on my sleeve like it's too obvious but then i remember how hard it is to tell someone i'd like to get to know them no fear at all unleashing the torrent of emotions i have in my soul but god forbid i make the object of my desires aware maybe that's a good thing the worst that can happen is i look like an idiot THE WORST THING THAT CAN HAPPEN IS I LOOK LIKE AN IDIOT honestly, my greatest fear, besides hurting another living being, is hurting my own pride and i LOVE apologizing but if it's to someone who won't hear it then god, i just made an ass of myself trying to fix something that means nothing these days sorry i stepped on your new shoes didn't see you there and now they hate me.
