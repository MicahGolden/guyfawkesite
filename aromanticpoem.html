a romantic poem for once

i'm an easy person to please too easy an ideal romantic outing for me is really just existing together somewhere nice a nice beer a bowl for two doesn't really matter to me i enjoy the odd adventurous evening one where we stroll along the trail no cell service so we get lost i look out on the sunset and say "isn't it beautiful" you say yes, you love sunsets and i say "i was talking about you" we picked the best day to come out because a torrent of rain falls from above our path is muddied for some reason we both wore our favorite shoes even though we're walking on a mix of mud and gravel but it's still the perfect evening home now, my pretentious persona is evoked and i say "records and wine?" who could say no to that. uncork the bottle, flip the record over watching some old movie feeling safe for once like i'm really good enough i fall asleep before the credits roll but that's how it was always going to end could there be a better day than this?
