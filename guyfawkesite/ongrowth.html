on growth

so many times people get complacent they get too comfortable in the way things are and aren't able to grow within the soil because, hey, it provides what it needs to a major fault in some human's minds the soil becomes a given "well duh, you'll always be here, you have to be!" but each time it rains the composition changes you begin to nurture someone who won't ever, never ever, understand that it's changed once uprooted they promise and swear they will but when that happens, it's because they're uncomfortable, without the consistency you're tired of being the soil maybe you want to be the flower for once and then you meet someone else willing to be your soil or maybe willing to grow alongside you but that's unfamiliar and scary so you find another plant to be soil to this time, they don't even want your brand but they keep you around "just in case" how is that fair? maybe your roots get entangled but you're drinking from the same water it's unhealthy for both of you but they don't care you need to realize that you need to grow side by side not entwined then and only then can you feel love
