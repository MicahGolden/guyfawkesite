another one about my values

i want to earn the right to settle down but before i get there i want to drink, smoke, fuck, love, love so fucking much that it's not our plan, it just ends up that way. "come home it's late" ha, remember when we'd stay out late? now i'm being asked to come home and as i drive home i have a cigarette i laugh at the juxtaposition "she used to love getting drunk, what happened?" then i look in the rearview at the aged bearded man that looks at me constantly "oh yeah, that's what" i wave goodbye to the man smoking a cigarette walk up the steps unlock the door arms folded, big chair "it's about time" "yeah yeah, i know" sloppy kisses positioned behind an enraged partner "sorry babe" "i love you too" sleep warmth sleep
