my box of teenaged angst

i open up one of the boxes, packed for me when i returned home inside are books upon books books i forgot i even had what's this one? a tattered spiral notebook ah, shit, it's this old thing my teenaged poems well, my memory is pretty solid but god, the words i wrote back then much more methodical like i had something to prove did i really feel this way? was love a robber-fly, melting my insides leaving me dead and hollow? at the time, i thought i was changing so much now i look at myself and don't recognize the boy behind the feelings back then hell, i listen to my old songs and barely recognize the words i guess the most important thing in life really is growth even if my writings are more dry the feelings behind them are more real maybe i'm happy to be my current self despite the pain it took to get here because it's worth it my friends say someday i'll get back what i tried to give and i sure hope that's true
