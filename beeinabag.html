bee in a bag

rustle rustle "what's that?" my sister asks strong hesitation from me, but I'm the older brother so I feign confidence I pour the packets upon packets of sauce out "it's a bumble bee!" his wing is hurt, crushed under the weight of packets upon packets "ah! what should we do?!" well, life is precious, and so is this little fuzzy fellow "let's take him outside, set him free!" I grab the paper bag, and there it is pour him out on the ground, making sure to be gentle he shakes, scared, definitely hurt "poor little guy" a moment passes and he flies away "goodbye, Mr. Bumblebee!" two days later by the pond flora buzz buzz buzz I could recognize that fluffy bumble from across the state good to know that Mr. Bumblebee is bumbling around must be a nice neighborhood
