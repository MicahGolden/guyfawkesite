artists anonymous

it's never a good thing to be inspired because when we're happy we don't feel the need to create to write to throw to draw because each stroke of the pencil or indentation in the clay is an outward expression of inner turmoil so when my friends show their art or i share a poem i know it hasn't been a great day it makes the finished piece damn good though honestly, i have a lot of imposter syndrome surrounding myself with people who can create more than a scribble on a bar napkin but i've learned to accept that my songs my writings are just as valid as their sculptures and paintings because it all comes from the same place the depths of a manic mind that beautiful thing so few understand. so few understand that even they can't make sense of it so we create
