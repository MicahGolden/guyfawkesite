motivation

any given week there's one day i have enough energy to really feel fine most days i force myself to get out of bed because i know it's what's best for me to see my friends to just go outside for a bit but i can't do it yet i do answering a text can be too much at times. depending on who it is, my response is carefully calculated not to say it's not genuine but there are so many possibilities i just want to choose the right one on that one day i let myself flow a little better live in the moment maybe it's because i have to and somehow the people i'm around seem to like me but any other day i think they hate me "maybe i'll just stay in this weekend" i say to myself but i never do and i always end up happier for having gone out but a part of me wonders what well is my drive coming from? will it run dry? is my motivation what'll happen next weekend? is it the next? or the next?
