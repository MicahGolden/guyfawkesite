dumb mailman

i ordered some ink a book and paper for my typewriter forgot to check the shipping address sent it back to the old place no hesitation i texted you to give it to a mutual friend a month ago i would be too scared too desperate to even attempt to talk to you but i guess i stopped caring took the time to so it doesn't hurt anymore funny how that works, huh? i really see you as just another body on this earth instead of the deity i had built you up as now, i actually feel nearly indifferent to you except the hurt you created in my life but that's all okay you always rejected the idea of keeping tabs but had a literal tab for me "because you'd do the same" bullshit, i'd have never done that shows how well you knew me shows how compatible we were the cynic, blind and refusing change miopic, unwilling to embrace the idea that two people can live separate lives, together because if it's not your life plan then it's nothing, yeah? i said i was indifferent, never said i don't have thoughts about the whole thing it just doesn't hurt to write about anymore
